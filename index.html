- For you, for the Earth

In today's world, we each face two giant challenges. One of them revolves around the one thing you always can have more of: money.

The second one is a slower, yet ever growing problem: climate change.
Climate change is all around us and harms our planet more and more every day, and money pays for everything but “everything” keeps costing more money. According to the WHO, seven million premature deaths each year are caused by air pollution, and those of you who are looking into the house market don't need a source to know that prices are out of control.

What if we could fix both of these problems?

Welcome to Terranomisma, your one-stop research station for technologies to improve your life and your wallet while also helping the environment. Get started by entering an amount of money you are willing to spend and the amount of time you are willing to wait before you make back your money, press "Find Technologies", and Terranomisma will take it from there. All figures are estimates and are unofficial. Click on pictures of technologies to go to websites we used to learn about them and you can, too. Our current database includes technologies such as rooftop solar panels, blown-in cellulose insulation, heat pumps, double-paned windows, and more. Get your start into a greener future with Terranomisma. Terranomisma: for You, for the Planet.
